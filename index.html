Our Story

Our journey began with the establishment of P&M Ads, a pioneering fabricator of billboard steel structures that quickly became a cornerstone for one of the country's leading outdoor advertising companies. Over time, P&M Ads expanded its expertise into construction, signage production, banner installation, and printing, laying a solid foundation for what we are today.

As our business evolved, we introduced JCPM Marketing Venture to cater to the digital and offset printing needs of our clients, ensuring a comprehensive advertising service offering. This was followed by the formation of Rhema Advertising, which took over from P&M Ads to serve a broader range of OOH advertising needs for our loyal customers across industries such as real estate, food, retail, and apparel. Rhema Advertising Co. (partnership) was then established to support our company's expansion and meet the growing demands of our client base, while JCPM Marketing Venture continued to focus on outdoor and indoor advertising sales.

A year after the country's strictest lockdown due to the global pandemic, TAV Outdoor Advertising emerged as the next chapter in our story. Replacing Rhema Advertising Co., we are equipped with a new business plan and an advanced system designed to better serve our customers' needs. At TAV Outdoor Advertising, we are committed to delivering cutting-edge advertising solutions and unparalleled service, ensuring your brand stands out in the ever-changing advertising landscape.

Join us as we continue to shape the future of outdoor advertising!
